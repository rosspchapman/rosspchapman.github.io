My Mentor Map

I was tasked with designing a mentoring app for students, staff and faculty to peer review and support mentoring within the school.

We reached a minimum viable product within 2 days and personally, it was, and still is, the most rewarding project I've ever worked on.

  • Duration and Team: 8 weeks, 1 designer, 1 developer and 1 product owner
  • Tools: Pen and Paper, Twitter Bootstrap, Keynote.
