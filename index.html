About Ross

I'm Ross Chapman, a product designer at Etch. I collaborate with businesses to gain a true understanding of their business objectives and in terms that I can understand. I kickstart the two-way conversation necessary between business and customer by designing products that improve over time through prototyping, testing and learning. Working this way is faster and smarter. I'm naturally impatient.

Before that, I was the Senior User Experience Designer at Wiggle, the online sports retailer. There, I defined the usability and interaction strategy of Wiggle's mobile and web app, delivering revenue improvements to the business with user-centered design. I'm probably not allowed to tell you by how much, but it was by a lot.

Recent collaborations

Wiggle (UX Design, User Testing), Ericsson Television (UX Design), Hult International Business School (UX Design, User Testing), Epilepsy Action (UI Design), UK Cycling Events (UX Design, UI Design), Mozzo Coffee (UX Design), Simrad (UI Design).
