About Ross

I am the Senior User Experience Designer at Wiggle, the online tri-sports retailer, where I define the usability and interaction strategy and propose solutions that promote user needs while considering business goals.

I was part of the UX team at Ericsson Television driving and communicating a vision for the user experience of broadcast television products.

With online academy CareerFoundry, I mentored students wishing to become user experience designers, with many students possessing no previous experience of UX design.

I worked at Hult International Business School as Lead UX designer, playing a critical role in defining and building the next-generation of web services at Hult.

I also ran a digital design studio called Skysoclear, where I provided design consultancy, user experience design and content design. I made sense of the data and offered insight as to how to make them even more effective to meet business goals and user needs.
