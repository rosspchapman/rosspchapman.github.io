Product Strategist + Digital Consultant with over 15 years of experience.

Ross is a product strategist, UX designer and consultant who helps organisations unlock the value of teams using design, fundamentally changing the way they operate.

He works with teams within businesses to execute strategies to work on the pressures that are facing the company and train people on better ways of solving problems at the same time.

Contact Ross if you need a new perspective to help you design new products, improve your operations, embed design thinking within your organisation, and help lead programmes of work to transform how you engage with customers in 2020.

Expertise

Ross has worked with brands including Land Rover, Save the Children and Wiggle. His focus is around collecting data and then executing solutions through strategy, digital and design.

This often means identifying what the team wants to achieve, prototyping ideas and validating them to ultimately create digital products, services and businesses that people truly value.

Key practices include:

  • Innovation & Ideation
  • Design Thinking
  • Problem Solving
  • Visual Collaboration & Remote Workshops
  • Experience Design
  • Business Strategy
  • Digital Consulting

Writing & Speaking

Ross has written articles for Net Magazine, Muzli, Prototypr, UX Planet and the InVision Blog.

He hosts the Etch Podcast, tackling subjects about product design, digital transformation and executing change within businesses.

Ross also speaks at events regularly, with previous talks including topics on collaborating with clients and what makes design possible within non-design focussed businesses.
